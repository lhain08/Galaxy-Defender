Galaxy Defender

Defeat the aliens and protect the Galaxy in this Galaga inspired game. Shoot down aliens and destroy bosses to protect the Galaxy. You are the last line of defense. Don't let the aliens get past you!

Instructions

Move with left/right arrows

Shoot with space bar

Collect coins and kill aliens and survive as long as you can

Use your coins and XP to unlock new ships and perks to help you survive longer
